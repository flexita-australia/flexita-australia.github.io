FAQs

What services do you provide?

Support Coordination, Support Workers and plan management. See the list of services we provide.

Do you provide both in-home support and community access?

Yes. We support you to achieve goal you have set yourself at home and in the community and to be as independent as possible.

Do you use interpreters?

Yes. We use interpreter through Translators and Interpreter Service (TIS) National at no charge to participants or participants funding.

Are you a registered provider?

Yes we are a registered provider.

Do you work with self-managed and plan managed participants?

We work with NDIA managed, self-managed and Plan managed participants.

About Us
Western Support Services is a registered NDIS provider, providing support services to participants with disabilities and we help ensure you live as independently as possible.
